About Cognicor AICTE IDEALab

AICTE in collaboration with COGNICOR established MEC IDEALab in 2022. It offers interactive learning workshops and training programmes with the goal of fostering creativity and promoting interest in applications of STEM, multidisciplinary education, and collaboration. It will enable faculty and students alike to "Engage, Explore, Experience, Express and Excel" by providing all the facilities required to turn an idea into a prototype and learn technical craft. It stands to assist in the development of commercially viable products, meet socioeconomic needs, and encourage entrepreneurship in the nearby student community.

Our Facilities

Epilog Laser Engraver

CO2 laser engraver for laser engraving, cutting, marking, and etching. Various sizes and wattages.

HMT CNC Lathe

CNC machine for cutting, sanding, drilling, and more with various tools.

HMT CNC Router Machine

Used for cutting shapes in wood, plastic, MDF, and metals.

PC Workstation

Desktop with Core i7 processor, 16GB RAM, 1TB SSD, and 3050Ti graphics.

PCB Manufacturing Station

Produces multi-layer PCBs and handles leaded and surface-mounted devices.

Sand Blasting Machine

Includes systems to drive sand, abrasive, and metal shot for blasting processes.

Shopbot CNC

Router for cutting wood, plastics, foam, and soft metals using included software.

Snapmaker

3-in-1 modular machine with 3D printer, laser cutter, and CNC engraver.

SolidWorks Software

Solid modeling CAD application for developing mechatronics systems.

Sublimation Printing Station

Transfers images onto various materials using a specific printing process.

Ultimaker 2+

3D printer with swappable nozzles and adjustable filament pressure.
